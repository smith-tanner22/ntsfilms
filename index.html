About Me

My name is Tanner, and videography has always been a treasured part of my life. From the moment I picked up my first camera, I’ve been creating videos, and now I’m fortunate enough to call it my career. Over the years, NTS Films has produced hundreds of videos in various genres, including weddings, bridals, business, sports, promotional, and real estate content. Our work has taken us to California, Arizona, Utah, and Idaho. We pride ourselves on delivering top-quality results at an affordable price. Above all, we truly love what we do, and we believe our passion shines through in every project.
